ABOUT US

Bengal AVGC-XR Association is a non-profit organisation committed to facilitate the development of the Animation, VFX, Gaming and Comics industry in West Bengal and in India.

It was formed by the stakeholders of all the major AVGC companies of Kolkata.

MISSION & VISSION

1.

SOCIAL MEDIA

Increasing our Social Media and Mass Media presence

2.

SEMINARS

Arranging Investor seminars to attract investment in the sector

3.

FOCUS

Facilitating in developing the curriculum currently followed in the training institutes to make them more industry-focused.

4.

BENIFITING

Lobbying with Central and State governments to generate financial / non-financial benefits for the member organisations.

5.

EVENTS

Organising events to generate awareness on AVGC

6.

AWARENESS

Organising master classes, seminars & conferences with reputed personalities of the AVGC segment across the globe to enhance knowledge & awareness levels of all stakeholders in the industry.
