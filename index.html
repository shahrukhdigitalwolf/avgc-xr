ABOUT US

Bengal AVGC-XR Association is a non-profit organisation committed to facilitate the development of the Animation, VFX, Gaming, Comics and extended reality AR-VR industry in West Bengal and in India.

It was formed by the stakeholders of all the major AVGC companies of Kolkata.

SOCIAL MEDIA
